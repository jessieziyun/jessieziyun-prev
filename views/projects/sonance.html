Sonance


Creative Coding, Virtual Reality

We see light, we hear vibrations, we feel pressure, and we taste and smell chemicals. For most, these ways in which we experience the world are discrete and fixed. But what if we could synaesthetically perceive our reality?

Sonance explores the union of sight and sound in an alternate dimension where sound is imagined as a plane of light.

Live app here

This project is designed to be viewed through a WebXR browser with a VR headset or Google Cardboard but may be viewed in 2D for a limited experience; feel free to navigate using your mouse, trackpad or touchscreen. Please enable microphone access to experience.
